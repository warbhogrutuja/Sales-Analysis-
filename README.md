# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis

An Excel-based data analysis project on **Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data**, built using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Excel formulas**. The goal of this project is to explore sales and order trends across months, demographics, geography, and sales channels, and to derive actionable business insights.

## 📊 Dashboard

[Vrinda Store Annual Report Dashboard](Dashboard.png)

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Excel Formulas (SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, percentage calculations, etc.)
- Slicers for interactive filtering (Month, Channel, Category)

## ❓ Sample Questions Explored
- Compare the sales and orders using a single chart
- Which month got the highest sales and orders?
- Who purchased more — men or women in 2022?
- What are the different order statuses in 2022?
- List the top 10 states contributing to the sales
- Relation between age and gender based on number of orders
- Which channel is contributing the maximum sales?
- Highest selling category?

## ✅ Answers / Key Findings

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Orders vs Sales trend | Both sales and order count rise from May through the year, peaking around April, with a noticeable dip around November |
| Highest sales & orders month | April recorded the highest sales and order count |
| Men vs Women purchases | Women purchased significantly more than men — **64% Women vs 36% Men** |
| Order status breakdown | **Delivered: 92%**, Returned: 3%, Cancelled: 3%, Refunded: 2% |
| Top 10 states by sales | Maharashtra (3.0M) leads, followed by Karnataka (2.6M), Uttar Pradesh (2.1M), Telangana (1.7M), Tamil Nadu (1.7M), Kerala (1.0M), Andhra Pradesh (0.9M), West Bengal (0.9M), Delhi (1.3M), Haryana (0.8M) |
| Age vs Gender (orders) | Across all age groups, women order more than men. Adults (30–49 yrs) contribute the most — **34.59% women vs 15.47% men** |
| Top channel by sales | **Amazon** contributes the most at **35%**, followed by Myntra (23%) and Flipkart (22%) |
| Highest selling category | Identified from the Category slicer/pivot — top category leads sales contribution (see workbook for exact %) |

## 💡 Sample Insights
- Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%)
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states (~35% combined contribution)
- The adult age group (30–49 yrs) is the maximum contributing segment (~50%)
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top 3 channels, contributing ~80% of total sales

## 🎯 Final Recommendation
To improve Vrinda Store's sales, the business should:
> Target **women customers** aged **30–49 years**, living in **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**, by showing targeted ads/offers/coupons on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** — the channels with the highest sales contribution.
